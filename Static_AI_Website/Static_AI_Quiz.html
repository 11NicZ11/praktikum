Solution 1

Question: Can AI completely eliminate bias in decision-making processes?

Answer: No, while AI can help mitigate bias to some extent, complete elimination of bias is challenging due to the potential biases in the data used to train AI algorithms and the complexity of human biases that can be embedded in decision-making processes. Therefore, it is important to develop robust strategies and approaches to address bias in AI systems and ensure responsible and fair decision-making
